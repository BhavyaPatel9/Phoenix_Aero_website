About

Find Out More About Phoenix Aero

Phoenix Aero Club is a technical student chapter at SVNIT, dedicated to advancing aircraft and drone technology. We provide a platform for aspiring engineers to gain hands-on experience, tackle real-world challenges, and excel in the field of aerospace. Our members actively participate in renowned competitions like SAE DDC, ADDC, and many other technical events. Additionally, we host exciting events such as Drone Helix and Carry the Containment, where students demonstrate their expertise in UAV navigation and design. Our flagship event, Aeroplus, features captivating airshows and interactive technology demonstrations, offering students and enthusiasts a unique opportunity to engage with cutting-edge aerospace innovations.

Find Out More About SVNIT

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology

Established in 1961 as one of India's Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs) with a focus on Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering. Over the years, it has expanded its academic offerings to include undergraduate programs in Electronics Engineering (introduced in 1983-84), Computer and Production Engineering (1988-89), and Chemical Engineering (1995-96). In 2002, SVNIT was granted "Deemed University" status under the UGC Act, recognizing its academic excellence. It further achieved the prestigious "Institute of National Importance" designation in 2007.

  • Vision Statement

    To be one of the leading Technical Institutes disseminating globally acceptable education, effective industrial training and relevant research output.

  • Mission Statement

    To be a globally accepted Centre of Excellence in technical education catalyzing absorption, innovation, diffusion and transfer of high technologies resulting in enhanced quality for all the stakeholders.

Today, SVNIT offers six undergraduate programs, nineteen postgraduate programs, and integrated MSc and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The institute is committed to providing world-class technical education, promoting innovative research, and serving as a hub for technological advancement.
