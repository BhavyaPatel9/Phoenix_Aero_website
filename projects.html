Aircraft Projects

Al VTOL

Al VTOL

This innovative RC aircraft design combines a multi-rotor fixed-wing hybrid configuration, providing excellent agility and flight efficiency. Its flying wing structure and blended body design reduce drag, improving aerodynamics for smoother , more stable flights. The aircraft is equipped with vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) capabilities, making it versatile for confined launch areas. Blended control surfaces enable seamless transitions between hover and forward flight modes, while the rear pusher configuration enhances fuel efficiency and reduces noise. With these advanced features, this RC aircraft is ideal for hobbyists and professional applications alike. The design is also protected by a patent, highlighting its cutting-edge approach to RC aviation.

Lakshya

Lakshya

This Regular Class RC aircraft design features advanced multi-element aerodynamics and an optimized fuselage for improved performance. The fuselage-wing interconnection is engineered with a gradient to minimize drag, enhancing overall flight efficiency. Semi-tapered wings contribute to better lift distribution and fuel efficiency, making the aircraft ideal for longer, more stable flights. The streamlined aerodynamic fuselage ensures smoother airflow, reducing resistance during flight. Additionally, the design incorporates a self-braking system, offering improved control during landings and reducing wear on components.

Garuda

Garuda

Our micro class aircraft is equipped with advanced features such as slotted flaps with wing fences for enhanced aerodynamic performance. The impact-resistant fuselage ensures durability and resilience, while the active stabilization system provides superior control and stability during flight. For added versatility, the design includes extendable features like a variable angle flap to adapt to different flight requirements and customizable winglet and fence combinations to optimize aerodynamic efficiency. These innovations make the aircraft highly adaptable and reliable, perfect for both casual enthusiasts and professional pilots.

Drone Projects

PZ-I

PZ-I

The PZ-I drone is a testament to advanced innovation and precision engineering, weighing just 2 kg while effortlessly carrying a 2.8 kg payload. With its fully aerodynamic design and autonomous capabilities, the PZ-I excels in tasks like surveillance and parcel delivery, operating seamlessly without human intervention. The drone features self-stabilization technology, ensuring steady flight even in challenging conditions. Equipped with intelligent systems like smart Return to Launch (RTL), it guarantees safe and accurate missions every time. This exceptional design earned the PZ-I a prestigious 3rd place in the Best Innovation category at SAE ADDC, showcasing its superior engineering and groundbreaking innovation.

Vaayu

Vaayu

Introducing our cutting-edge drone, a 2kg marvel designed for precision and versatility. With a lifting capacity of 3kg, it excels in both surveillance missions and the delicate task of fragile medical parcel delivery. Powered by high-torque T motors and guided by a state-of-the-art 6C Pixhawk flight controller, this drone ensures stability and accuracy in every flight. Equipped with a servo-based payload dropping mechanism and advanced sensors for real-time parcel condition monitoring, it represents the pinnacle of drone technology. This innovation was recognized as one of the top 10 at the SAE Aero Design Challenge (ADDC) 2023.
